The story

Yes, there is one.

An ancient cosmic mother — the First Udder — once wandered the universe, and her milk became the oceans, rivers, and clouds of every world. When she rested, her essence scattered into bloodlines across the cosmos. Her descendants still produce trace amounts of her power: Star Milk. The Lacteans, an alien empire, have been hunting bloodline carriers for millennia. They came for the cow. She fled — and discovered she's not the last of her kind.

Two heroes, two biomes

v1 launches with two protagonists, deliberately chosen to feel different in your hands.

Biome 1 · Farm

The Cow

Milk arc projectiles · aim & shoot

Tap and pull the udder to fire arcs at incoming Lacteans. The pull distance controls power, the angle controls the arc. A bright, twilight farm full of escalating chaos — chickens, dogs, and a UFO that should not be there.

Biome 2 · Desert

The Camel

Pressurized chargeable spit · charge & release

Hold to build pressure. Release for variable-damage spit. Quick taps for pip-shots; long holds for boss-piercing pressure jets. Rust-and-sunset palette, hostile wildlife, and a different rhythm of combat from the farm.
