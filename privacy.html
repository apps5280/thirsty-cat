Thirsty Cat

Privacy Policy

Effective May 18, 2026

Thirsty Cat is an arcade game for iOS. We've designed it to need as little of your data as possible — almost none, in fact. This page explains exactly what does and doesn't happen with your information when you play.

What we collect

Nothing. Thirsty Cat does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers. We don't have servers.

The game saves your progress (current wave, high score, unlocked content, settings) locally to your device using iOS's standard UserDefaults. That data never leaves your device.

Game Center

If you sign in to Game Center, the game uses Apple's Game Center service to submit your scores to public leaderboards and award achievements. Game Center is operated by Apple — its handling of your data is governed by Apple's privacy policy, not ours.

Game Center is optional. The game works fully without signing in; you simply won't appear on leaderboards or earn achievements.

In-app purchases

If Thirsty Cat ever offers in-app purchases, transactions are handled entirely by Apple. We don't receive your payment information, Apple ID, billing address, or any other identifying detail — only an anonymous receipt indicating which item was bought, which the game uses to unlock that content on your device.

Analytics, ads, third-party SDKs

The game contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs. We don't know how often you open the app, which biome you're playing, how far you got, or where in the world you are. We've made a deliberate choice not to instrument the game for these things.

Children

Thirsty Cat is rated 9+ for mild cartoon violence (a cow squirting milk at alien invaders). Because we don't collect any personal information from anyone, we don't collect any personal information from children either. The app is COPPA-compliant by default.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how the game handles data, we'll update this page and revise the "Effective" date above. Material changes will also be noted in the game's release notes.
